The Local Problem

Where waiting is not just a line—it's lost livelihood.

Across India, queuing is one of the most persistent public pain points. In large government hospitals (like AIIMS), registrar offices, regional public banks (like SBI), and college administrative counters, thousands of citizens crowd waiting rooms every morning.

These places are characterized by shouting operators, confusing paper registers, and massive anxiety. Citizens must stand in physical queues for hours simply because the system cannot explain itself. The uncertainty is draining—people have no idea when their number will be called, if they can step away for a glass of water, or what specific documents they must prepare.

For low-wage workers, a hospital or bank visit represents a lost day of wages. For senior citizens or patients, it is physically demanding. For administrative staff, dealing with frustrated, crowded rooms leads to operational bottlenecks and error-prone triage.

"In Indian outpatient departments, people queue from 5:00 AM. 40% of the friction is not because of slow doctors, but because patients do not understand where to go next, what files are missing, or when their turn actually is."

Dr. Harish Rohith, SaaS Founder
The SaaS Vision

Our Innovation: SkipQ as a Multitenant SaaS Enterprise

SkipQ goes beyond simple queue numbering. By combining high-speed caching engines with Google's advanced Generative AI, we've designed a robust B2B SaaS platform specifically optimized for the complex demographics of Asia Pacific.

Multitenant Admin Console

Hospitals, banks, and universities sign up and spin up custom branches, specific desks, and multiple service queues in under five minutes.

Smart Triage & Aadhaar Flow

Customers join securely using phone numbers or quick ID sweeps. SkipQ checks requirements and routes users to the correct desk instantly.

Inclusive WhatsApp Sync

Low-literacy and low-connectivity citizens receive standard WhatsApp cards. Gemini status logs are pushed directly as messages, bypassing heavy web interfaces.

Regional Language Engine

Gemini translates complex counter instructions into 12+ major Indian languages, assuring accessibility for every non-English-speaking visitor.

High-Performance FIFO

Engineered using Bun, Elysia, and Redis. Manages high-concurrency ticket checkouts, automated customer no-show benches, and active buffers.

AI-Guided Queue Triage

Gemini contextually assesses queue variables (e.g., waiting size, staff load, student scholarship deadline) to deliver tailored customer action plans.

Targeting the Heart of Digital Inclusion

India is a mobile-first country, yet high-speed data connectivity can be inconsistent, especially inside thick-walled public concrete buildings. Furthermore, digital literacy varies extensively.

SkipQ bridges this digital divide. By utilizing **Gemini’s ultra-compact semantic processing**, we translate heavy status metrics into standard offline notifications:

When a citizen scans a QR code at an SBI Counter or government hospital, they can opt to close the browser entirely. SkipQ's **WhatsApp Integration Layer** routes dynamic text cards, keeping the user updated on queue status, wait times, and instructions via SMS and WhatsApp.
